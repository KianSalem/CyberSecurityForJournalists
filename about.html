About Us

Cybersecurity for Journalists is a project created by a group of cybersecurity professionals and journalists with a mission to educate and empower journalists to protect their digital assets and sources. Our team is dedicated to providing up-to-date and relevant information on the ever-changing landscape of cybersecurity.
